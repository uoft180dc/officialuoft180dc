180 Degrees Consulting is the world's leading provider of high quality consulting services for non-profits, social enterprises and socially minded companies.

Each year we work with several hundred organizations to help them overcome challenges they’re facing and increase their social impact. We’ve worked with organizations as large as Red Cross and World Vision, right through to small start-up social enterprises.

We help organizations to...

  • Run the most effective programs possible
  • Measure their social Impact
  • Improve their financial sustainability
  • Operate as efficiently as possible, so that donation revenue goes further
  • Be moredata-drive in their decision-making
  • Expand to new geographical areas
  • Improve their human resource management and volunteer
  • Improve their branding and marketing
  • Work out organizational priorities
  • Increase the number of people they are able to help
  • Develop a workable business plan

Being selected to receive consulting services is a competitive process. Typically less than 30% of organizations that apply for consulting services receive it.

We Ensure Consulting Quality in the following ways:

Rigorous Application Process

In order to be selected as a 180 Degrees Consultant, applicants need to (a) have a very strong academic record, (b) prove relevant experience and/or ability, and (c) go through one or more interviews to ensure that their motivations and values are consistent with those of 180 Degrees Consulting.

Quality Training

Our consultants receive both general and project-specific training from world leading top-tier consulting companies. Many of our 180 Degrees Consultants also received mentoring from full-time consultants with decades of professional experience.

International Network

180 U of T is one of 88 branches internationally. 180 Degrees Consulting has had engagements with international non-profits such as Red Cross and World Vision. What makes 180 Degrees Consulting different is that we are able to utilize resources from our international network to make a difference in the local communities.

Specialization and Diversity

On consultant team can consist of a Masters student at the Munk School of Global Affairs, an Industrial Engineering undergraduate student, and a Marketing student from the Rotman School of Management. The diversity of our consultants allows us to specialize for any particular engagement.

Checks on Quality

Each consulting project is led by a Team Leader, who is overseen by a Branch Consulting Director, who is then in turn managed by an International Consulting Director. This three-tier quality control process ensures our work is always of the highest standard.

University of Toronto Resources

When you recieve consulting services from 180 UofT, you also recieve consulting services from resources at the University of Toronto. Our consultants utilize (a) advisement from the expert faculty at the University of Toronto, and (b) as students we have access to particular resources provided from the school.

Expert Board of Advisors

Even though we have a high-performing executive team at 180 UofT, all branch-wide decision making is reviewed and rigorously thought through with our expert board of advisors consisting of community leaders and faculty members.

Expansive Alumni Resources

The alumni of 180 Degrees Consulting - University of Toronto go on to work for top-tier companies, successfully launch start-ups, and attend graduate school in Ivy League universities. We have access to the guidance and expertise of our overachieving alumni.

Toronto is our Home

Every consultant, committee member, and executive member of 180 Degrees Consulting is here on a volunteer-basis. The reason we commit our early morning weekends and light nights on mondays to the work is because each member believes they are making a difference. Toronto is our home and we strive to make our mark and see the legacy we leave behind.

We are always accepting new requests for our consulting services. We are reviewing our engagement wait lists and time lines in the near future.
